Omelette

Description

This is an omelette aux fines herbes.

Ingredients

  1. 3 Small Eggs (600g) or 2 x 700g eggs
  2. 1 Egg white - optional
  3. 1 Tablespoon Butter
  4. 1 Tablespoon Finely grated Gruyere cheese
  5. 1 Tablespoon Herbs - any mixture of parsley, chives, tarragon, chervil
  6. Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare your equipment
  2. Cook
  3. Serve
